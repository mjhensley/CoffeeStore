Where It All Began

In 2018, a group of passionate coffee enthusiasts came together with a simple mission: source the finest beans from around the world and roast them to perfection. What started as a small operation has grown into something special.

At a time when mass-produced coffee dominated the market, Grainhouse took a different path. We believed that great coffee should taste like the place it came from ‚Äî bright, complex, and alive with flavor. Today, you can taste our specialty coffee beans shipped fresh from our roastery.

Direct Trade

From the beginning, we knew that exceptional coffee starts at origin. That's why we pioneered the direct trade model ‚Äî building long-term relationships with farmers, paying premiums well above commodity prices, and investing in the communities that grow our coffee.

Today, we work with producers across Latin America, Africa, and Indonesia. Try our Ethiopia Mordecofe from Sidama's high-altitude farms, or explore all our single origin coffees.
