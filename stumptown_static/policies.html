Privacy Policy

At Grainhouse Coffee, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, shipping address, billing address, and phone number when you place an order or create an account.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details are processed securely through our payment processor Snipcart and are never stored on our servers.
  • Communication Preferences: Email subscription preferences and communication history.
  • Order History: Records of your purchases, subscriptions, and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and delivery
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and receipts
  • Manage your subscription and account preferences
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Send promotional emails (only if you've opted in)
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Detect and prevent fraud

We never sell your personal information. Your data is only shared with trusted third parties necessary to fulfill your orders (shipping carriers, payment processors) and is protected under strict confidentiality agreements.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand user behavior. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure payment processing, and regular security audits to protect your information.

Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@grainhousecoffee.com.

Shipping & Delivery

We ship fresh roasted coffee from our roastery directly to your door. All orders are roasted within 1-3 business days and shipped promptly to ensure maximum freshness.

Free Shipping: Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders over $45 within the continental United States!

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-3 business days. During peak seasons or promotions, processing may take up to 5 business days. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Shipping Destinations

  • Domestic: We ship to all 50 US states. Alaska and Hawaii may have extended delivery times.
  • International: We currently ship to Canada. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.
  • PO Boxes & APO/FPO: We ship to PO Boxes and military addresses via USPS.

Subscription Shipping

Subscription orders receive free standard shipping regardless of order value. Subscriptions are shipped on a recurring schedule based on your selected frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also view your order status by logging into your account.

Delivery Issues

If your package is lost, damaged, or significantly delayed, please contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue or send a replacement.

Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we're here to make it right.

100% Satisfaction Guarantee: If you're not satisfied with your coffee for any reason, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll provide a replacement or full refund — no questions asked.

Coffee Products

Due to the perishable nature of coffee, we cannot accept returns of opened bags. However, if you're unhappy with your coffee:

  • Contact us within 30 days of receiving your order
  • Let us know what went wrong — we love feedback!
  • We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund at your preference

Brewing Equipment & Gear

Non-coffee items may be returned within 30 days of delivery if:

  • The item is unused and in its original packaging
  • All tags and accessories are included
  • You have proof of purchase

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or defective:

  • Take photos of the damage
  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • We'll send a free replacement immediately

Subscription Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Cancellations made at least 48 hours before your next billing date will stop the upcoming shipment. Shipments already processed cannot be cancelled but are eligible for our satisfaction guarantee.

How to Request a Return or Refund

To initiate a return or refund, please:

  • Email us at support@grainhousecoffee.com
  • Include your order number and reason for the return
  • Our team will respond within 24 hours with next steps

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of approval. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank.

Exchanges

We don't offer direct exchanges. If you'd like a different product, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.
